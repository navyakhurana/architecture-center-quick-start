<tr><td style={{border: 'none', paddingTop: '16px', paddingBottom: '16px', paddingRight: '32px', verticalAlign: 'top'}}>AI-native</td><td style={{border: 'none', paddingTop: '16px', paddingBottom: '16px'}}>Architecture where AI is designed into the core experience, process, foundation, and platform layers, rather than added as isolated features.</td></tr>
<tr style={{background: 'var(--ifm-table-stripe-background)'}}><td style={{border: 'none', paddingTop: '16px', paddingBottom: '16px', paddingRight: '32px', verticalAlign: 'top'}}>AI-first</td><td style={{border: 'none', paddingTop: '16px', paddingBottom: '16px'}}>AI capabilities embedded into existing products or workflows, but still bounded by application, data, and governance silos.</td></tr>
<tr><td style={{border: 'none', paddingTop: '16px', paddingBottom: '16px', paddingRight: '32px', verticalAlign: 'top'}}>Autonomous Enterprise</td><td style={{border: 'none', paddingTop: '16px', paddingBottom: '16px'}}>SAP's vision for enterprises that use AI assistants, agents, connected data, and governed automation to operate across business domains with less manual coordination.</td></tr>
<tr style={{background: 'var(--ifm-table-stripe-background)'}}><td style={{border: 'none', paddingTop: '16px', paddingBottom: '16px', paddingRight: '32px', verticalAlign: 'top'}}>System of record</td><td style={{border: 'none', paddingTop: '16px', paddingBottom: '16px'}}>The transactional foundation that stores business facts, enforces rules, and records what happened.</td></tr>
<tr><td style={{border: 'none', paddingTop: '16px', paddingBottom: '16px', paddingRight: '32px', verticalAlign: 'top'}}>System of context</td><td style={{border: 'none', paddingTop: '16px', paddingBottom: '16px'}}>An intelligence layer that connects data, process knowledge, decision history, interactions, and semantics so agents can reason with business context.</td></tr>
<tr style={{background: 'var(--ifm-table-stripe-background)'}}><td style={{border: 'none', paddingTop: '16px', paddingBottom: '16px', paddingRight: '32px', verticalAlign: 'top'}}>Cognitive Core</td><td style={{border: 'none', paddingTop: '16px', paddingBottom: '16px'}}>The intelligence foundation formed by business data, semantic knowledge, reasoning models, agents, and platform services.</td></tr>
<tr><td style={{border: 'none', paddingTop: '16px', paddingBottom: '16px', paddingRight: '32px', verticalAlign: 'top'}}>Context engineering</td><td style={{border: 'none', paddingTop: '16px', paddingBottom: '16px'}}>The practice of assembling the right authorized, current, and relevant context for an AI interaction.</td></tr>
<tr style={{background: 'var(--ifm-table-stripe-background)'}}><td style={{border: 'none', paddingTop: '16px', paddingBottom: '16px', paddingRight: '32px', verticalAlign: 'top'}}>Harness engineering</td><td style={{border: 'none', paddingTop: '16px', paddingBottom: '16px'}}>The practice of wrapping models and agents with enterprise controls such as identity, policy, sandboxing, memory, observability, evaluation, and governance.</td></tr>
<tr><td style={{border: 'none', paddingTop: '16px', paddingBottom: '16px', paddingRight: '32px', verticalAlign: 'top'}}>Specification engineering</td><td style={{border: 'none', paddingTop: '16px', paddingBottom: '16px'}}>The practice of defining agent behavior, constraints, success criteria, and evaluation measures precisely enough to test and improve outcomes.</td></tr>
<tr style={{background: 'var(--ifm-table-stripe-background)'}}><td style={{border: 'none', paddingTop: '16px', paddingBottom: '16px', paddingRight: '32px', verticalAlign: 'top'}}>Agentic orchestration</td><td style={{border: 'none', paddingTop: '16px', paddingBottom: '16px'}}>Coordination of agents, tools, APIs, events, data products, and workflows to decompose goals, act, observe results, and adapt.</td></tr>
<tr><td style={{border: 'none', paddingTop: '16px', paddingBottom: '16px', paddingRight: '32px', verticalAlign: 'top'}}>Managed agent runtime</td><td style={{border: 'none', paddingTop: '16px', paddingBottom: '16px'}}>A platform-managed environment for deploying and running agents with sandboxing, identity, memory, observability, evaluation, and tenant isolation.</td></tr>
<tr style={{background: 'var(--ifm-table-stripe-background)'}}><td style={{border: 'none', paddingTop: '16px', paddingBottom: '16px', paddingRight: '32px', verticalAlign: 'top'}}>Agent identity</td><td style={{border: 'none', paddingTop: '16px', paddingBottom: '16px'}}>A first-class digital identity that lets an agent authenticate, receive scoped authorizations, act under policy, and be audited.</td></tr>
<tr><td style={{border: 'none', paddingTop: '16px', paddingBottom: '16px', paddingRight: '32px', verticalAlign: 'top'}}>Data flywheel</td><td style={{border: 'none', paddingTop: '16px', paddingBottom: '16px'}}>A self-reinforcing loop where richer data improves AI models and decisions, AI-driven decisions improve processes, and improved processes generate even richer data.</td></tr>
<tr style={{background: 'var(--ifm-table-stripe-background)'}}><td style={{border: 'none', paddingTop: '16px', paddingBottom: '16px', paddingRight: '32px', verticalAlign: 'top'}}>Federated Knowledge Graph</td><td style={{border: 'none', paddingTop: '16px', paddingBottom: '16px'}}>A model where domain-specific knowledge graphs retain autonomy while connecting through shared enterprise concepts.</td></tr>
<tr><td style={{border: 'none', paddingTop: '16px', paddingBottom: '16px', paddingRight: '32px', verticalAlign: 'top'}}>Semantic grounding</td><td style={{border: 'none', paddingTop: '16px', paddingBottom: '16px'}}>Anchoring AI outputs in metadata, domain models, data definitions, authorization rules, and enterprise semantics to improve accuracy.</td></tr>
<tr style={{background: 'var(--ifm-table-stripe-background)'}}><td style={{border: 'none', paddingTop: '16px', paddingBottom: '16px', paddingRight: '32px', verticalAlign: 'top'}}>Sovereign AI</td><td style={{border: 'none', paddingTop: '16px', paddingBottom: '16px'}}>AI architecture and operations designed around data residency, regulatory, infrastructure, model, and operational sovereignty requirements.</td></tr>
<tr><td style={{border: 'none', paddingTop: '16px', paddingBottom: '16px', paddingRight: '32px', verticalAlign: 'top'}}>Human-in-the-loop</td><td style={{border: 'none', paddingTop: '16px', paddingBottom: '16px'}}>A control pattern where humans review, approve, override, or guide AI actions for high-risk or high-impact decisions.</td></tr>
